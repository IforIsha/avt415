Soléa Sustainable Sunglasses

Soléa is an eco-conscious eyewear company that creates stylish sunglasses made entirely from recycled ocean plastics and biodegradable materials. Each pair is thoughtfully designed to combine fashion with environmental purpose, empowering wearers to protect the planet while expressing their individuality. Soléa offers a range of sunglasses in various colors—Amber, Ocean Blue, and Charcoal Black—and frame styles including Classic, Round, and Oversized. Complementing the eyewear collection are eco-friendly accessories such as recycled fabric sunglass cases and soft microfiber cleaning cloths, reinforcing the brand’s commitment to sustainability and mindful design.

Ember & Pine Handcrafted Candles

Ember & Pine offers hand-poured soy candles inspired by the tranquility of nature, designed to bring warmth and calm to everyday spaces. Each candle is crafted with eco-friendly wax and presented in reusable glass jars, reflecting the brand’s dedication to sustainability and simple elegance. The collection features soothing scents such as Lavender Grove, Forest Ember, and Citrus Bloom, each evoking a unique natural atmosphere. Ember & Pine also provides candle gift sets and refill kits, allowing customers to enjoy their favorite fragrances while reducing waste and embracing mindful living.

Lume Modern Desk Lamps

Lume designs minimalist, energy-efficient desk lamps that combine clean aesthetics with practical functionality. Each lamp is crafted from recycled aluminum and features dimmable LED technology, offering both sustainability and modern style for any workspace. Available in a range of sizes—Mini and Standard—and colors such as Matte Black, Sandstone, and Mustard, Lume’s lamps are made to complement diverse interior styles. For added convenience, customers can also choose an optional USB charging add-on, making Lume the perfect blend of form, function, and eco-conscious design.
